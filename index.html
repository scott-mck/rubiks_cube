How to play:

MOUSE INSTRUCTIONS
Click on face and drag to move
Click off face and drag to rotate

KEYBOARD INSTRUCTIONS
Space: scramble
i, k: move right face
f, j: move up face
e, d: move left face
s, l: move down face
g, h: move front face
q, p: move back face
a: look left
semi-colon: look right
y: look down
n: look up
Enter: auto-solve!
